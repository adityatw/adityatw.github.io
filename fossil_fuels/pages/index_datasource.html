Data Sources


The following are the data sources used in the analysis:


  • Global Fossil Fuel Consumption:
    https://ourworldindata.org/fossil-fuels - (first chart - download CSV data)
  • Share of primary energy consumption from fossil fuels - G7 and individual countries:
    https://ourworldindata.org/fossil-fuels - (sixth chart - download CSV data)
  • Coal reserve:
    Coal and coke - (select Download Options - 'Export CSV (table)')
  • Oil reserve:
    World proven crude oil reserves by country - (select Table 3.1 and click on Download Selected)
  • Natural gas reserve:
    Natural gas - (select Download Options - 'Export CSV (table)')
