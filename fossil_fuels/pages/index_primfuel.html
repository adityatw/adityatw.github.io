Percentage of primary energy consumption from fossil fuels by country


  • On this page, you can explore the trend of energy consumption from fossil fuels in different countries around the world. Use the drop down selector to choose the country you are interested in.
    Not every country has data for the full year range.
  • An interesting observation is a comparison among three island nations New Zealand, Samoa, and Singapore, where you will see very different profiles. Is this a reflection of their energy policy?
  • Use your mouse to brush horizontally to zoom-in a year range. Double-click to reset the view.
